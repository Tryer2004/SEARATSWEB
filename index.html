About Sea Rats

Founded in 2024, Sea Rats competes in the SeaMate ROV competition. After winning all Scout-level awards, the team is competes at the Scout level, focusing on ocean monitoring and climate mitigation. We are still in the Scout level because we need to devolop our team more.
