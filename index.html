This site will describe what and how the Sea Rats work!(this site is in beta and will have frequent updates)

The Sea Rats are a company created back in 2024. They were founded to compete in the SeaMate ROV competition. In the annual competition, they won all awards at the Scout level and now compete in the Ranger level.Our theme in the 2025 season for SeaMate was Monitering and Mitigating the ocean.Lets analyse this text, Monitering means to look and check, and mitigating means to stop.This means this years theme is about watching and putting an end to climate change.This website was created as a personal try at coding, and if you want to try coding and want to create your own website, my advice is to not give up and keep going.During the SeaMate competition we only scored a total of 115 points throughout 2 trys, But we got the most points from the presintition and our Marketing Display (which we worked on the most), which made us win the competition.During the competition we were missing our collegue Cody Simpson and we were behind schedule,but we were able to do so much work by 1. staying afterschool to work on it and 2. putting lots of effort everyday for 1 week.
