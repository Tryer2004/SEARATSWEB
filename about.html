About Sea Rats

Sea Rats is an underwater robotics team founded in 2024 at Miami Country Day School as part of the Underwater Robotics program.

Our Mission

Our mission is to design, build, and operate underwater robots that support ocean monitoring and environmental protection while developing real-world engineering skills.

SeaMate Competition

In the 2025 Florida Regional SeaMate competition in Saint Petersburg, Sea Rats won all Scout-level awards. The team now competes at the Ranger level with a focus on monitoring and mitigating ocean damage.

Our ROV

Our robot, the Sea Scavenger, was designed for stability, maneuverability, and reliability. It uses closed-cell foam for buoyancy and a custom claw optimized for efficient object retrieval during missions.
