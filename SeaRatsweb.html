The Sea Rats are a company created back in 2024/2025. They were founded to compete in the SeaMate ROV competition. In the annual competition, they won all awards at the Scout level and now compete in the Ranger level.Our theme in the 2025 season for SeaMate was Monitering and Mitigating the ocean.Lets analyse this text Monitering means to look and check and mitigating means to stop.This means this years theme is about watching and putting an end to climate change.
